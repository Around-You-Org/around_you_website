Our Story

We're Building the Future of
Local Services

AroundYou was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves fast, reliable access to skilled professionals nearby. We're making that a reality across Nigeria.

Our Mission

To make quality local services accessible to everyone by connecting people with vetted, reliable professionals through smart technology — creating economic opportunity for service providers while delivering convenience and trust to customers.

Our Vision

To become Africa's most trusted hyperlocal services platform — a world where finding skilled, reliable help is as simple as opening your phone, and where every artisan and professional has access to a thriving digital marketplace.
